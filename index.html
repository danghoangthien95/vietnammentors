Vietnam Mentors Initiative (VMI)

Vietnam Mentors Initiative (VMI) is an effort following the open discussion among mentoring programs in Ho Chi Minh city facilitated by SECOEP in August 2016. With the participation of mentoring programs, and ecosystem actors those offer mentoring services to entrepreneurs and startups, Vietnam Mentors Initiative aims at

Vietnam Mentors Initiative (VMI)

Developing mentor capacity by providing mentors and to-be mentors with zero-cost training courses. People who are interested in the courses can join freely. They just have to commit to provide a certain amount of time in a year to deliver voluntary mentorship to startups introduced by members of VMI. Different courses will be designed to meet different level of experience of mentors.

Developing a mentor community by sharing and leveraging resources among organization members such as mentorship manuals, mentorship skill materials, joint mentorship programs, and the likes..

Honoring mentors in the ways that are well acknowledged by the startup ecosystem and the public audience.

VMI become the most effective and well-connected mentor community in Vietnam.
